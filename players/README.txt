DROP YOUR PLAYER IMAGES HERE
============================

Save these 4 files in this folder:

  kohli.jpg       — Virat Kohli (cricket, India)
  tendulkar.jpg   — Sachin Tendulkar (cricket, India)
  ronaldo.jpg     — Cristiano Ronaldo (soccer, Portugal)
  chhetri.jpg     — Sunil Chhetri (soccer, India)

Tips for best results:
  - Portrait orientation (taller than wide) looks best
  - Full-body or upper-body shots work well
  - High resolution (at least 600×900 px)
  - The image will be edge-faded automatically, so centered subjects work best

Supported formats: .jpg  .jpeg  .png  .webp
(if you use .png or .webp, update the src= paths in SportBackground.jsx)

Where to find free licensed images:
  - Wikimedia Commons (commons.wikimedia.org) — many CC-licensed sports photos
  - Search "[player name] wikimedia commons" for available options
